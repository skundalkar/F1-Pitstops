# Pitwall Planner

An enthusiast-first Formula 1 strategy and driver-lineup planner.

Pitwall Planner will turn public race data, qualifying results, tyre choices,
circuit characteristics, and weather into explainable pre-race plans and
in-race replanning scenarios. It is a decision-support tool: recommendations
will show their assumptions, uncertainty, and the events that would change the
call.

## First release

- Build a driver lineup for an upcoming Grand Prix.
- Compare conservative, aggressive, and weather-contingency tyre strategies.
- Simulate outcomes and understand the reasoning behind each recommendation.
- Save a shareable race brief for teammates.

Live race support will begin as a clearly labelled, public-data second-screen
experience after the pre-race simulator and historical replays are validated.

## Product principles

1. Decisions, not data dumps.
2. Explain every strategic call and its trigger.
3. Show uncertainty honestly.
4. Prioritize current race context over historical averages.
5. Serve both quick race briefs and deep analysis.

## Status

Project foundation. No historical race data has been ingested yet.
